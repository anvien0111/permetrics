From now on, :math:`y_{pred}` is the estimated target output, :math:`y_{true}` is the corresponding (correct) target output.
:math:`Y_{pred}` is the whole estimated target output , :math:`Y_{true}` is the corresponding (correct) target output.
:math:`mean(Y_{pred})` is the mean of whole estimated target output , :math:`mean(Y_{true})` is the mean of whole (correct) target output.
